Ayurveda dosha questionnaire

This questionnaire serves to determine your dosha. These are the elements that make up our body, consisting of the six elements air, ether, fire, water, water and earth. When doshas are out of balance, we won't perform optimally e.g. feel overwhelmed, tired or fall sick. Vikruti is the set of imbalances currently in our body and mind and will change over time as our bodies are in constant change. Prakriti is our natural doshic constitution and restoring our vikruti back to this essential constitution is one of the key principles in ayurveda.

This questionnaire seeks to help you find your tendencies, i.e. your doshic constitution. To better understand prakriti tendencies, think of what you were like when you were younger, or for the most part of your life.
